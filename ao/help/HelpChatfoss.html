ChatFOSS is a Safe Place for Everyone

ChatFOSS is a safe place online where familes and friends, including both adults and children, can enjoy instant messaging, photo sharing and video sharing in complete privacy. In ChatFOSS, no one can find you, no one can see who your other friends are and all your information remains yours only. ChatFOSS does not sell your data, carries no advertising and there are no in-app purchases.

ChatFOSS employs military grade security technology throughout, including: transport layer security, AES-256 encryption (both for data transfer and for files held in storage), EU-located servers, fully protected data centers and strict corporate governance, access controls, policies and procedures.


Using ChatFOSS

You can use ChatFOSS on any web browser or by downloading the ChatFOSS app, available at the Apple, Google Play and Amazon app stores.

Recent, Circles, Friends

When you log in, the first page you see shows a summary of all recent activity from all your friends, with the most recent at the top. You can select any item to see the underlying content or to begin communicating with that friend or circle. The active friend or circle is displayed in the top left of the centre area of your screen.

Select "Circles" to see a list of all your groups of friends. Select a circle name to make that circle active and to see a list of all recent activity from that circle.

Select "Friends" to see a list of all your friends. Select a friend to make that friend active and to see a list of all recent activity from that friend.

My Area is your private space in ChatFOSS. It is for information that is private to you, that you do not wish to share or that you might wish to share later.

Chat

Select "Chat" to send an instant message, image or other file to other members of the active channel.

Safe Store

This is each channel's secure, private storage area. Members of a channel can view, upload, download and organise photos and other files here.

Email

You can also send emails to the channel. All messages are logged and stored in the channel history. Emails are forwarded onto the email accounts of the other members of the channel, or if a member has a phone number rather than an email, notifications are sent via SMS.

New

Select "New" to invite new friends to chat or join you in a circle. Simply follow the on-screen instructions.

Account

Click on your name top right to see "Account" to change your password and other personal details.
