About ShareFilesNOW

ShareFilesNOW provides a private and professional network for file-sharing, email and notes. All communication is stored securely.

ShareFilesNOW can be accessed from any internet-enabled computer, tablet or mobile phone, via internet browser, app or telephone. It works with existing systems, including email and calendars.

ShareFilesNOW enables you to ensure that all your information is at all times correctly organized and is shared between only those with whom it should be shared. Note that when you share information with representatives of a company, the company itself will be able to access your information (using administrator access rights). The way information information stored wihin the system is shared by a company is entirely a matter for that company.

ShareFilesNOW employs market-leading security technology throughout, including: transport layer security, AES-256 bit encryption (both for data transfer and for files held in storage), EU-located servers, Tier 4 data centers, strict corporate governance, access controls, policies and procedures.


Using ShareFilesNOW

You can access ShareFilesNOW through most modern web browsers or using the Secure Portal app on your iOS or Android mobile device or tablet. The interface is necessarily arranged slightly differently depending on the device and screen resolution, and certain features (including parts of Account Management) are not available on mobile devices or tablets, but your experience of the system should be similar across all devices.

Dashboard

When you log in, the first page you see is your "Dashboard". This contains a summary of all recent activity from all your contacts, with the most recent at the top. You can click on each item to see the underlying content. When you click on an item you are taken to the contact with which that activity is associated. The active contact is displayed in the top left corner of your screen. Once a contact is active you can start communicating with that contact.

Vault

The vault is the active contact's secure, private storage area. You can view and download files stored here. Depending on your access rights, you can upload new files, create new folders, move and delete files.

To add a new file to the Vault, go into the Vault, click on the "Add" button at the bottom of the screen, and choose "Upload File". To create a new folder, click on the "Add" button and choose "Create Folder".

Send Email

You can send either emails or SMS's to your contacts. All messages are logged and stored in the history. Emails are forwarded onto the email accounts of your contacts. SMS's are sent to the mobile phone numbers of your contacts.

Make Recording

Click on "Make Recording" to record a voice message to share with other members of the group.

New

Here you can set up new users and groups. Simply follow the onscreen instructions.

Schedule

You can use Schedule to email a meeting request or a reminder. The request or reminder will be sent within the system and forwarded to the email inboxes of the group members in a format that enables them to save it directly into their existing calendar.

Account

Here you can change your password, your email address and other personal details.
