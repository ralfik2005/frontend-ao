About VideoMeetingsNOW

VideoMeetingsNOW is a cloud-based service that provides a secure, private and professional network for group video and telephone calls, live file-sharing, screen-sharing, notes, audio recording and messaging. All communication is stored securely.

VideoMeetingsNOW can be accessed from any internet-enabled computer, tablet or mobile phone, via internet browser, app or telephone. It works with existing systems, including email and calendars.

VideoMeetingsNOW enables you to ensure that all your information is at all times correctly organized and is shared between only those with whom it should be shared. Note that when you share information with representatives of a company, the company itself will be able to access your information (using administrator access rights). The way information information stored wihin the system is shared by a company is entirely a matter for that company.

VideoMeetingsNOW employs market-leading security technology throughout, including: transport layer security, AES-256 bit encryption (both for data transfer and for files held in storage), EU-located servers, Tier 4 data centers, strict corporate governance, access controls, policies and procedures.


Using VideoMeetingsNOW

You can access VideoMeetingsNOW through most modern web browsers or using the Secure Portal app on your iOS or Android mobile device or tablet. The interface is necessarily arranged slightly differently depending on the device and screen resolution, and certain features (including parts of Account Management) are not available on mobile devices or tablets, but your experience of the system should be similar across all devices.

Dashboard

When you log in, the first page you see is your "Dashboard". This contains a summary of all recent activity from all your contacts, with the most recent at the top. You can click on each item to see the underlying content. When you click on an item you are taken to the contact with which that activity is associated. The active contact is displayed in the top left corner of your screen. Once a contact is active you can start communicating with that contact.

Make Call

To join or begin a conference video call, click on "Make Call". Depending on your settings, you can choose whether to record the call or not. (VideoMeetingsNOW only records audio, not video.)

At the bottom right of your screen you will see settings enabling you to turn off and on your video, mute and unmute your mic or pause (temporarily turn off your mic and video).

Screen Sharing

If another participant in a meeting is sharing a screen you will see a small version of their screen super-imposed over the top left of their video. Click on this image and their shared screen will swap places with their video feed, allowing you to see their screen more clearly, and making their video feed smaller.

Share Screen

During a call you can share a window from your computer screen by clicking on "Share Screen". This will bring up a list of available windows to share. Click on the one you wish to share to start sharing this window live. The shared window will remain active on the other participants' screens until the end of the call or until cancelled.

Screen Sharing in Chrome

Later versions of the Chrome browser by default do not allow plug-ins. VideoMeetingsNOW does not need a plug-in to work in Chrome, with one exception: if you wish to share your screen, you must use the VideoMeetingsNOW plug-in (you can still see other users' screens without the plug-in). To enable the plug-in, please follow the following steps. Firstly, enable NPAPI by going to the following URL: chrome://flags/#enable-npapi in your Chrome browser, and under "Enable NPAPI" select "Enable". Secondly, restart Chrome. Thirdly, log back into VideoMeetingsNOW. Fourthly, within the video set up process (which should start automatically once you log back into VideoMeetingsNOW) choose "Advanced Options" and deselect the final option to allow screen sharing in Chrome.

Chat Board

To send an instant message, image or other file to other members of the active group, click on "Chat Board".

Make Recording

Click on "Make Recording" to record a voice message to share with other members of the group.

New

Here you can set up new users and groups. Simply follow the onscreen instructions.

Schedule

You can use Schedule to email a meeting request or a reminder. The request or reminder will be sent within the system and forwarded to the email inboxes of the group members in a format that enables them to save it directly into their existing calendar.

Account

Here you can change your password, your email address and other personal details.
